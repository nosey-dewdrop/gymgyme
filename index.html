// the problem

every gym app tracks sets.
none track neglect.

you log your workouts. you feel productive. meanwhile, half your body hasn't been touched in weeks and you don't even notice.

01

the leg day loop

you train legs every session and forget everything else exists. your shoulders haven't been touched in 3 months. no app tells you.

02

memory is garbage

what was your leg press PR? how many reps? at what weight? gone. every session starts from zero because you forgot where you left off.

03

atrophy is brutal

5 days off can undo 5 months of work. if you haven't trained a muscle group in X days, something should scream at you. nothing does.

04

motivation drain

seeing stagnation without context kills motivation. seeing actual progress — weight going up, PRs being hit — makes you want to go back.

// how it works

start in 60 seconds

no account needed. no onboarding quiz. no paywall.

1

add your exercises

type them in or search the wger exercise database. assign muscle groups. that's your library.

2

log your workouts

tap an exercise, enter weight and reps. gymgyme shows your last session so you know where you left off.

3

watch the colors

muscle groups turn yellow, then red, then go dark as you neglect them. atrophy becomes visible.

4

build programs

create workout programs from your exercise library. gymgyme tells you what muscle groups you're missing.
