# Ideas

- cardio tracking with duration (running, cycling, swimming etc) - not everything is sets/reps
- yoga and pilates support with duration-based tracking
- body map visual - tap where you want to train instead of reading anatomy terms
- human-friendly muscle names: "glutes" not "gluteus maximus", "butt" not "posterior chain". regular people dont know anatomy
- calendar view for workouts - see what you did, plan what you'll do
- more visuals everywhere - people are visual, show dont tell
- the app feels too complex for casual users, needs to be more approachable
- timer/stopwatch for duration-based exercises
